Post-module activity — Learning & Development

Use this tracker for one full working week after completing the Time Management module. Log each task as you go, assign it to a priority quadrant, and review your patterns at the end of each day. The goal is honest self-awareness — understanding where you're spending your time will allow you to make more intentional choices about how you work.
